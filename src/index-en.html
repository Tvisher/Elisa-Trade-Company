About company

Elisa trade company s. r. o. (IC 26379309, Czech Republic) is a large trading company specializing in the trading of hydrocarbons. One of the activities is the supply of thermal coal mined in the territory of the Republic of Kazakhstan, under a long-term supply contract with KazKomir Company LLP.

KazKomir Company LLP is a member of an international group of companies engaged in the mining, processing and enrichment of coal of various types in the territory of the Republic of Kazakhstan .

KazKomir Company LLP is a member of an international group of companies engaged in the mining, processing and enrichment of coal of various types in the territory of the Republic of Kazakhstan . Coal mining is carried out by the enterprises of Transkomir LLP (License No. 15011839 dated June 23, 2015) and Edelweiss LLP (Contract No. 932 dated May 15, 2002). The annual volume of coal mined is about 1.5 million tons. In Karaganda, Kazakhstan, in 2018, an enrichment plant was built and put into operation in order to enrich our own and third-parties’ coal.

The company's management has many years of experience in supplying coal both to the domestic market and for export. The shipment of coal is carried out on its own with the involvement of its own / rented wagons. Elisa trade company s. r. o. is a supplier of products for large metallurgical enterprises, cement plants, the energy sector, etc. Taking into account significant changes and restrictions in the supply of coal to the EU, Elisa trade company s. r. o. promptly responded to new requirements, and in order to meet consumer demand, it began mining and production of thermal coal in the Republic of Kazakhstan. We have also worked out various logistics multimodal supply chains that meet all requirements, which allows us to offer integrated solutions for the supply of coal to various markets.
